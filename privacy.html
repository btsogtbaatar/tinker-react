Privacy Policy for Seed Mobile App

Effective Date: January 18, 2025

At Seed, we are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how it is used, and the steps we take to safeguard it when you use our mobile application ("App"). By using Seed, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use the Seed app:

  • Personal Information: When you create an account, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, and location (if applicable).
  • Social Media Authentication: If you sign up with Google or Facebook:
    • Google: Name, email address, profile picture, and any other data you allow us through Google OAuth.
    • Facebook: Name, email address, profile picture, and other data you permit via Facebook OAuth.
  • Service Usage Data: Details on how you interact with the app (e.g., service requests, searches, interactions).
  • Location Data (Optional): If enabled, we may collect your location to help match you with nearby service providers or requestors.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve services, including matching requestors and providers and processing service requests.
  • Manage authentication and user accounts.
  • Communicate with you (e.g., updates, notifications, promotional emails — you may opt out).
  • Analyze and enhance app performance and functionality.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. However, we may share information in the following cases:

  • With service providers: To fulfill your service requests.
  • With third-party services: For authentication and account linking only (e.g., Google, Facebook).
  • Legal requirements: When required by law or legal process.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. If deleted, we may retain some information for legal or business purposes for a reasonable period.

5. Security of Your Information

We implement reasonable measures to protect your data. However, no digital system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

6. Your Choices

  • Manage your data: You can update or delete your info via app settings.
  • Third-party access: Manage OAuth permissions in your Google or Facebook account.
  • Marketing preferences: Opt out of promotional emails through links or settings.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted in the app and on this page with a new “Effective Date.”
